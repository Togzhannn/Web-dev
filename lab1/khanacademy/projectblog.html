First Impressions of Seoul

Posted on January 25, 2026

Arriving in Seoul was like stepping into the future. The subway system is incredibly quiet and efficient. My first stop was Gyeongbokgung Palace, where I watched the Royal Guard Changing Ceremony. The contrast between the silent, colorful palace and the busy glass skyscrapers of Gwanghwamun is something you have to see to believe.

N Seoul Tower Views

Posted on January 27, 2026

Today I hiked up Namsan Mountain to reach the N Seoul Tower. Even though it was a bit chilly, the panoramic view of the Han River winding through the city was worth it. I saw thousands of "love locks" attached to the fences. Afterward, I headed down to Itaewon for dinner, which is a great place to find a mix of international and traditional Korean food.
