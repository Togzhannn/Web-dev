Recipe #1: Dumplings

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people (Makes about 25–30 dumplings)
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Pork 300g
Tofu 1/2 block
Chives 1 cup

Step 1: In a large bowl, mash the dry tofu and mix it thoroughly with the ground pork and chopped chives.

Step 2: Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper; stir until the mixture becomes slightly sticky.

Step 3: Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of a wrapper, dampen the edges with water, and fold it in half, pinching the edges to seal.

Step 4: Heat a pan with oil to pan-fry until the bottoms are golden, or steam them in a basket for 10 minutes.

Recipe #2: Bibimbap

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Steamed Rice 2 bowls
Spinach 150g
Gochujang Paste 2 tbsp

Step 1: Sauté vegetables like carrots, spinach, and mushrooms individually in a pan with a little oil.

Step 2: Fry an egg sunny-side up.

Step 3: Place the rice in a large bowl and arrange the cooked vegetables neatly on top.

Step 4: Add the egg and a large spoonful of Gochujang, then mix everything before eating.

Recipe #3: Kimchi Pancake

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chopped Kimchi 1 cup
All-purpose Flour 1/2 cup
Water 1/4 cup

Step 1: Mix the chopped kimchi, flour, and water in a bowl until it becomes a thick batter.

Step 2: Add a splash of kimchi juice for extra color and flavor.

Step 3: Pour the batter into a hot oiled frying pan and spread it out thin.

Step 4: Cook for 3-5 minutes on each side until the edges are crispy and brown.
