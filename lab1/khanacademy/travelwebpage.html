Travel to South Korea, Seoul

Reasons why you should visit Korea

Seoul - is a top-tier travel destination in 2026 because it combines ancient history with extreme futurism. Here are the most useful, bite-sized reasons to visit:

  • Ancient Palaces: Explore Gyeongbokgung (1395) or the UNESCO Secret Garden.
  • World-Class Food: Taste real Korean BBQ, Street Food (Gwangjang Market), and aesthetic Seongsu-dong cafes.
  • Super Tech: Experience the world's fastest 5G/6G, robot-run cafes, and the 123-story Lotte World Tower.
  • K-Culture: Visit the source of K-Pop, K-Dramas, and the global K-Beauty skincare headquarters.

Best Time to Visit: April (Cherry Blossoms) or October (Autumn Foliage).

If you only have time for three dishes on your first night, these are the absolute "Big Three" experiences for any traveler in Seoul: Korean BBQ (Samgyeopsal), Chimaek (Fried Chicken & Beer), Bibimbap.
